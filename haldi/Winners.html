Cheers to the Champions of TTU!

In the spirit of excellence and sportsmanship, these awards shine a spotlight on the outstanding athletes, dedicated teams, and inspiring coaches who have tirelessly contributed to the rich tapestry of sports at TTU. From heart-stopping moments on the field to the unwavering commitment behind the scenes, our inaugural Sports Awards are a celebration of triumphs both big and small, fostering a sense of camaraderie and pride that unites us all.


Join us on this thrilling journey as we pay homage to the athletes who have showcased unparalleled skill, the teams that have exhibited exceptional teamwork, and the coaches whose guidance has propelled our sports community to new heights.Get ready to be captivated by the stories of perseverance, the joy of victory, and the spirit of sportsmanship that define the essence of the Takoradi Technical University Sports Awards. We invite you to explore, engage, and share in the excitement of this groundbreaking celebration of sporting excellence.
